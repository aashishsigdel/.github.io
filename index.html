About Us

Tucked away in the heart of Kathmandu City, Padma Dental emerges as a sanctuary for all your oral health needs. Led by a team of highly experienced dentists and supportive staff, our clinic offers a comprehensive range of dental services, from routine dental check-ups to advanced implant procedures. Our state-of-the-art digital X-rays, other equipments and techniques ensure precise diagnosis and meticulous treatments. Trust us for a radiant smile and optimal oral health care.
